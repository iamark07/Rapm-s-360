Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our services, pricing, and more.

We offer a comprehensive range of laundry services including regular washing, dry cleaning, ironing, steam pressing, and express services. We also provide pickup and delivery options for your convenience.

Our pricing is based on the type of service and weight of clothes. We accept cash, credit/debit cards, UPI, and digital wallets. You can also pay through our mobile app for a seamless experience.

We offer pickup and delivery services from 8:00 AM to 8:00 PM, Monday through Saturday. You can schedule a pickup through our app, website, or by calling our customer service.

We have specialized processes for delicate items, including silk, wool, and designer wear. Our trained staff follows specific care instructions and uses appropriate cleaning methods for each fabric type.

Regular service takes 24-48 hours, while express service is available for same-day delivery. Dry cleaning typically takes 48 hours. We also offer special rush services for urgent requirements.
